Honey production in US

Price per lb trend: The X axis represents the year (independent variable) and the Y axis represents the price per lb (dependent variable) of honey per year in lbs. Line chart with different colors represent the price per lb per state. A line chart was chosen here since year is a continuous variable. Different colors were added to different state since its a nominal data. To understand the impact of cost lets look at the Price per lb trend line chart. It is evident that there was a gradual increase in price per lb for all states from 1998-2012. So, this corroborates that cost played a role in reduction of honey production. Infact one striking observation is that for states OK & SC the price per lb increased drastically by 2003, the same year they ceased production as well

Note: Only the top 7 & bottom 2 states based on total production are chosen else the charts were very crowded
