Total production: To show the states with the highest and lowest honey production from 1998-2012, a map has been used which provides an overview as to the relative honey production among the different states in US. The saturation as been used to identify the highest (ND) & lowest (SC) honey production states (cumulative of 1998-2012) & these two states have been labeled with the total production. Details for the other states will show up in tooltip. From the Total production map, we can clearly see ND & SC were the highest and lowest producer of honey cumulative of years 1998-2012.
