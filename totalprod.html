Total production trend: From the chart below it is evident that the honey production of most of the states had a trend for reduced honey production from 1998-2012 (i.e. both before & after the year 2006). This implies there could be another explanation to the decreased production other than the suspected "Colony Collapse Disorder" in 2006. Since the imports increased one potential reason could be the cost of production which may be higher domestically as compared to imports. We will explore this more in the next slide.
